Manti

Description

Manti is a dish made by putting minced meat seasoned with various spices into small pieces of dough and boiling these pieces in water. Manti, which is a part of Turkish cuisine as well as Central Asian cuisine, is also a popular food in the former USSR countries.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of flour
  • 1 glass of warm water
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 egg
  • 250g minced meat
  • 1 medium onion
  • Half a teaspoon of black pepper
  • Half a teaspoon of chili pepper
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • Garlic yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon mint
  • Half a teaspoon of sumac

Steps

  1. Let's add 3 cups of flour and 1 teaspoon of salt to a deep mixing bowl. Let's mix and create a hole in the middle of the flour.
  2. Let's add 1 egg in the middle and continue mixing.
  3. While mixing, slowly add 1 cup of warm water.
  4. Knead until the dough reaches a consistency that is neither too hard nor too soft.
  5. Cover the dough with stretch film and let it rest.
  6. While the dough is resting, add 250 grams of low-fat ground meat to a suitable mixing bowl.
  7. Then add 1 medium-sized onion, half a teaspoon of black pepper, 1 teaspoon of salt, half a teaspoon of chili pepper and mix well.
  8. Take the dough to the counter that we have sprinkled with flour, knead it and gather the dough. Divide the dough into 3 equal parts.
  9. Make the dough we have separated into balls. Roll out the balls we have prepared on the counter that we have sprinkled with flour, one by one, with a rolling pin so that they are thicker than yufka.
  10. Cut the dough we have rolled out into small squares with a knife. Put small pieces of the ground meat mixture we have prepared in the middle of the squares we have cut.
  11. Bring the 4 corners of the dough together in the middle and give it the shape of manti. Let's gather the manti we shaped on a tray whose bottom we floured and freeze it by putting the tray in the freezer.
  12. You can store the manti we took from the freezer in double-lock bags to cook later in the freezer. Let's boil water in a suitable pot.
  13. Let's add the manti we took from the freezer to the water and cook for 15-20 minutes. Let's add 2 tablespoons of butter to a suitable sauce pan, then add 2 tablespoons of tomato paste and mix well.
  14. Let's add the tomato sauce we prepared to the cooked manti and continue cooking while mixing. Let's put the cooked manti in a deep plate and pour garlic or regular yogurt on it. Our manti recipe is ready. You can serve it by adding red pepper flakes, mint and sumac.
